Frequently Asked Questions
How do I connect my Shopify store?
Navigate to Integrations from the sidebar, click the Shopify card, and follow the OAuth flow. Your store data will sync automatically every 2 minutes.
Can I export data to CSV or Excel?
Yes. On any table in the dashboard, look for the Export or CSV button in the card header. You can also export via the API.

Is my data encrypted?
All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We never share or sell your data to third parties.
How do I switch between light and dark mode?
Click the moon/sun toggle in the top-right header. Your preference is saved in your browser's local storage.
What plan do I need for API access?
API access is available on the Pro plan ($79/mo) and above. Enterprise customers get enhanced rate limits and dedicated endpoints.
